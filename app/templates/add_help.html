Source

URL

The YouTube channel or playlist to subscribe to. Three forms are accepted:

https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
https://www.youtube.com/@channelhandle
https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLxxxxxxxxxx

Channels are checked via RSS (15 most recent videos). Playlists are checked via --flat-playlist and support date_after filtering.

Name

Display label used in the UI, logs, and archive filenames. Keep it short with no slashes or special characters.

Default: derived from the URL.

Output directory

Where downloaded files are written inside the container. Must start with /downloads/ — the prefix is added automatically if omitted.

/downloads/ngslot

Default: /downloads/<name>

Schedule & Quality

Interval (hours)

How often the subscription is checked for new videos. Use 1 for active channels; 6 or 24 for slower ones.

After saving, run Stagger Schedules from the Job Status tab so all subs in the same interval group don't fire simultaneously.

Default: 6

Quality

Maximum video resolution. yt-dlp picks the best available format at or below this height, preferring MP4+M4A.

14402K — recommended for most channels 10801080p HD 720 720p — smaller files 480 SD — audio-primary channels bestuncapped — use with caution on 4K channels

Default: 1440

Filters

date_after

Playlists only — skip videos uploaded before this date. Has no effect on channel (RSS) subscriptions. Accepts absolute dates or relative expressions:

20250101 Jan 1 2025 and later today-7days last 7 days today-30days last 30 days today-6monthslast 6 months today-1year last year

Default: none (download entire playlist history).

Min duration (seconds)

Skip videos shorter than this many seconds. Useful for filtering out clips and highlight reels on channels that mix long and short content. Set to 0 to disable.

Default: 180 (3 minutes)

Exclude Shorts

Skip YouTube Shorts — vertical short-form videos detected via URL pattern /shorts/. Leave on for most subscriptions.

Default: on

Exclude Live

Skip videos currently airing as a live stream — these can't be downloaded in final form yet. Scheduled premieres ("Premieres in…") are also skipped.

Default: on

Exclude Past Live

Skip videos that were live streams but have since ended and been archived as replays. Turn off only if you specifically want live stream replays from a channel.

Default: on
